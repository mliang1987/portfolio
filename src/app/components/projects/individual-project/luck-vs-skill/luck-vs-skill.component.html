In August 2020, Veritasium, a popular YouTube personality, posted a video on whether success was determined by luck or hard work. The video is linked below.

There were significant problems with the assumptions made in Veritasium's video. Most notably, they assume that luck and skill follow a flat distribution. Changing this small assumption to a normal distribution drastically changes the thought experiment results. In the following video and accompanying repository, I demonstrate these changes.

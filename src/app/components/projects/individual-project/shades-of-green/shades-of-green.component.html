As the world becomes ever more environmentally-conscious, farmers will need smart tools that can differentiate between crops and weeds in order to facilitate spot-application of pesticides. This is either a manual, labor-intensive operation or can be automated through the magic of machine learning. In this project, we look at a data-set of crop and weed seedlings during their nascent phases, and try to create a machine learning classifier with >90% accuracy.
